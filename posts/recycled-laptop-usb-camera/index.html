Recycled Laptop Usb Camera

2020-09-17
#hardware 

for a little side project, i needed small usb cams, preferably for free. i still had old hp elitebooks 8440p, and from their display, i removed the camera, see the picture below. the picture shows always the pinout.

next, i soldered the 4 cables together, leaving the GPIO pin unused. no big deal, there is no faster way to get an usb-cam for raspberry and co.

Pin Name Wire color Description
1 V(Bus) Red +5 V
2 D- White Data-
3 D+ Green Data+
4 GND Black Ground
