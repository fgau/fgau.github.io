Blind Guardian in Berlin

2022-09-29
#music 

my first concert after(!) the seemingly endless pandemic. and it was as expected: very good atmosphere in a very good location with very good sound a very good band from krefeld downtown.

how can you describe this band? i think, as a very good long player, as a well aged whiskey. this evening, blind guardian was in excellent playing mood. the guardian’s celebrated the 30th anniversary of the album “Somewhere Far Beyond” and other classics from the band’s history. what a wounderful concert on sunday night, love this metal genre too!
